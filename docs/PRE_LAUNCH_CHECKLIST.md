# ✅ Pre-Launch Checklist

Use this checklist before launching your painting & plastering website to ensure everything is ready!

---

## 📝 Content Review

### Company Information
- [ ] Company name updated (both EN and VI)
- [ ] Tagline/slogan reflects your business
- [ ] Hero headline is compelling
- [ ] Hero subtitle accurately describes your services
- [ ] All placeholder text replaced with real content

### Contact Information
- [ ] Phone number is correct and clickable
- [ ] Email address is correct and clickable
- [ ] Physical address is accurate
- [ ] Business hours are current
- [ ] All contact info matches in both languages

### Services
- [ ] All 4 service descriptions are accurate
- [ ] Service titles match what you offer
- [ ] Descriptions are clear and compelling
- [ ] Translated properly to Vietnamese

### Statistics & Trust Indicators
- [ ] Project count is accurate (e.g., 1000+)
- [ ] Award/certification is real (or removed if not applicable)
- [ ] Guarantee period is correct (9 months)
- [ ] Property types match your business (Residential/Commercial)

### Testimonials
- [ ] Customer names are real (or changed to protect privacy)
- [ ] Reviews accurately reflect your service
- [ ] Customer roles/types are diverse
- [ ] All testimonials are in both languages
- [ ] 5-star ratings are appropriate

## 🆘 Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue: Images not loading
**Solution:** Check image URLs, ensure they're publicly accessible

### Issue: Language toggle not working
**Solution:** Clear browser cache, check console for errors

### Issue: Mobile menu not opening
**Solution:** Test on actual mobile device, not just resized browser

### Issue: Links not working
**Solution:** Check for typos in URLs, ensure `href` attributes are correct

### Issue: Content overlapping
**Solution:** Check responsive CSS, test at different screen sizes
